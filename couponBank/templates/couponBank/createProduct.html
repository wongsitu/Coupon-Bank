How does it work?

Thanks to Google Cloud Vision API, we are able to extract the brand and description of the coupons and gift cards you upload here. It uses Convolutional Neural Networks which works by breaking down the image by filters, these are just smaller groups pixels. By applying many filters, the CNN looks for specific patterns of the image and does a series of calculations to recognize it.

We decided to use this technology with the main focus of automizing tedious and boring tasks that most users have to go though when trying to sell. Because the development of e-commerce is increasing, the user experience has to improve too.
